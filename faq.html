How do I use my subsidy account?

On every litre purchased, ₦2 - ₦10 is deducted from your subsidy account as discount on price per litre. Discount varies with the amount of money you have in your subsidy account. This means the higher the balance in your subsidy account, the higher the discount.

How do I fund my wallet?

You can fund your wallet by using your card.

Is my card information safe?

Remis is not a payment platform and for this reason we do not keep your card information. All payments made on Remis passes through the security measures implemented by flutter wave.

Can Remis be used in any station?

Currently Remis can be used in 150+ partner stations within Nigeria

How do I earn subsidy balance?

You earn ₦500 free subsidy top-up when you refer a friend to download and register the Remis application

How can I locate Remis partner stations?

On Remis Mobile App the station locator let's you access google maps direction to any partner stations
